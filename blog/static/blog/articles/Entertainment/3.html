A recent video posted on twitter went viral describing an Indian family that were caught by the staff of a hotel in Bali for stealing hotel accessories. The incident took place in Bali, Indonesia when the family were leaving the hotel.
In the 2.20 min video, the hotel staff is shown recovering items like towels, decorative pieces, electronics and other stuff.
Twitterati has reacted very strongly against the family and have even demanded that there passports must be canceled.
